## Complaints

### Latency
- **[user-reported]** Informal benchmark: Together AI was slowest of three tested — **2.60s** vs. Fireworks 1.42s and Groq 1.28s (Llama-3-70B). _(rkwasny, 2024-04-23)_ — [source](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=40129707)
- **[user-reported]** Described as "cheap but **slower**" vs. Groq/Fireworks; also surprise monthly bills juggling providers (posted by a competing router). _(jadendhm, 2026-02-24)_ — [source](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47137266)
- **[user-reported, competitor]** Competitor benchmark claimed **588 tok/s vs. Together AI's 298** on the same VLM workload. _(vshah1016, 2026-03-12)_ — [source](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47355410)

### Pricing
- **[user-reported]** Argued Together AI's low token prices are "heavily **subsidized by more than $1B in VC money**," raising true-cost/sustainability concerns. _(Denzel, 2026-06-09)_ — [source](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48467489)

### Docs / Features
- **[user-reported]** Said Together AI "**lacked some key features**" for prompt engineering — prompt history, parameter adjustments, and result/cost comparison (said while launching a tool built on top of it). _(lokinchiandy, 2024-05-17)_ — [source](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=40387824)

### Other (business-practice allegation — unverified)
- **[user-reported, allegation]** A "Tell HN" post alleged Together AI is tied to a proof-of-work GPU "mining" scheme (via a $PRL/Pearl partnership) the author calls "snake oil and vaporware." Unverified opinion. _(secondary_op, 2026-06-06)_ — [source](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48422809)
