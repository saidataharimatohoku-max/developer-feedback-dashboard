# Together AI — Public Developer Feedback (as of 2026-06-26)

> Sourced from publicly available posts. Each item is **user-reported** and links to its source. Window: last 365 days (live refresh plus a few older notable items).
>
> **Sources queried:** Hacker News (Algolia public API), the official Together AI status page, and a GitHub issues sweep. **Reddit, Trustpilot, and G2 blocked automated requests (HTTP 403)** and were not included. This is a **merged** dataset — the original HN sweep plus a 2026-06-26 live refresh.

## Complaints

### Latency
- **[user-reported]** In an informal benchmark, Together AI was the **slowest of three providers** tested (2.60s vs. Fireworks 1.42s, Groq 1.28s) on a Llama-3-70B request. _(rkwasny, 2024-04-23)_ — [source](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=40129707)
- **[user-reported, competitor]** Described as "cheap but **slower**" vs. Groq/Fireworks; also surprise monthly bills juggling providers (posted while launching a competing router). _(jadendhm, 2026-02-24)_ — [source](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47137266)
- **[competitor benchmark]** A competitor claimed **higher throughput** than Together AI on the same VLM workload (588 tok/s vs. 298 tok/s). _(vshah1016, 2026-03-12)_ — [source](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47355410)

### Pricing
- **[user-reported]** Argued Together AI's low token prices are "**heavily subsidized by more than $1B in VC money**," raising true-cost/sustainability concerns. _(Denzel, 2026-06-09)_ — [source](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48467489)
- **[user-reported]** Said Together AI is a good US provider but **~15x more expensive** than DeepSeek's subsidized pricing — "not that attractive at that price point." _(pants2, 2026-05-11)_ — [source](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48090537)
- **[user-reported, competitor]** Raised true-cost/sustainability concerns, arguing low headline prices may rely on **burning investor cash**. _(booty, 2026-05-19)_ — [source](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48199304)

### Downtime
- **[user-reported]** A user said Together AI seems "fairly unreliable with **constant outages or higher than normal latency**." Corroborated by the status page's sub-100% 60-day uptime. _(HDBaseT, 2026-05-19)_ — [source](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48200664) ✅ verified
- **[status page]** Official status page shows reduced 60-day uptime on several inference models — **Google Gemma 4 31B IT at 97.869%**, plus GLM 5.2 (99.101%), Qwen3.5 235B (99.227%), DeepSeek V4 Pro (99.587%) — despite all services reading "operational." _(status.together.ai, 2026-06-26)_ — [source](https://status.together.ai/) ✅ verified

### Docs / Tooling
- **[user-reported]** Reported Together AI "lacked some key features like prompt history, parameter adjustments, and the ability to compare different prompt results and costs directly" (said while launching a tool built on it). _(lokinchiandy, 2024-05-17)_ — [source](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=40387824)

### Other
- **[unverified allegation]** A "Tell HN" post alleged Together AI is tied to a proof-of-work GPU "mining" scheme (a $PRL/Pearl partnership), calling it "snake oil and vaporware." Opinion/accusation, not confirmed fact. _(secondary_op, 2026-06-06)_ — [source](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48422809)

## Context (non-complaint mentions seen)
