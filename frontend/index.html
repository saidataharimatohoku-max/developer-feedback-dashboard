Data collected from public developer forums — GitHub issues, Stack Overflow, Hacker News and the providers' official status pages. Items are auto-collected and unverified; counts reflect volume of feedback, not severity.

Provider health

A heuristic concern index per provider, driven by the complaint rate (the share of that provider's own feedback that is a complaint — so bigger providers aren't penalised just for having more collected posts), plus the share that is downtime and recent momentum. Higher = more developer concern. A ▲ marks a recent spike vs. the trailing average.

Heuristic and relative — a comparison aid, not an official reliability rating. Complaint rate is a proportion of collected public feedback, not a per-user rate (provider user counts are not public).
